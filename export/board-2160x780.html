[ 01–02 · RESEARCH PLATES ]

What the project leans on

Red tape is not malice — it is mechanism. Divided permission, mandatory records, bounded remits: each rule is sensible alone; combined, they turn a simple request into something referred, supplemented, escalated, archived — not necessarily resolved.

[ 04 · FINDING ]
Whatever bureaucracy appears, appears on its own.
F1With every condition on, a simple request gathers referrals, memos and supplementary demands — nobody was told to do any of this.
F2Switch hierarchy, paper trail or memory off one at a time, and the matching behaviors thin out with them.
F3A lone agent, handed the same matter, simply answers it.
